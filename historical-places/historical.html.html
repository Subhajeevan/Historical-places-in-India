Taj Mahal

One of the most famous historical sites in India, the Taj Mahal of Agra dates back to the 17th century. It was built by Shah Jahan, the Mughal emperor, in the memory of Mumtaz Mahal, his third wife.

Red Fort

When it comes to the famous historical places in India, one cannot miss the Red Fort in Delhi. Also popularly known as the Lal Qila, the Red Fort was constructed during Shah Jahan’s reign in the 17th century.

Khajuraho Temple

The famous Khajuraho Temples in Madhya Pradesh are amongst the top 10 historical places in India, popular for being home to a total of 85 Hindu temples.

Golden Temple

The top 10 historical places in India also include the world-famous Golden Temple, located in the city of Amritsar in Punjab. Also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, the Golden temple is the most important religious site

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal in Jaipur is one of the best historical places in India, in addition to being a prominent landmark of Rajasthan. Dating back to the 18th century, Hawa Mahal is a 5-storey structure that overlooks the bustling streets of Jaipur.

Victoria Mahal

Ranking amongst the top 10 historical places in India, Kolkata’s Victoria Memorial was constructed in honour of Queen Victoria, making it one of the relics of the British Raj. Built entirely out of white marble.
